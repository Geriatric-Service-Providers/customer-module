Best Of Health care services

“Palliative Care Services ” aim to provide care such as home nursing services . The goal is to improve quality of life for both the patient and the family. Palliative care is provided by a specially-trained team of doctors, nurses and other specialists who work together with a patient’s other doctors to provide an extra layer of support. It is appropriate at any age and at any stage in a serious illness, and it can be provided along with curative treatment. The services of the service providers will be listed and published. The user can filter the service on the basis of location and service type.
